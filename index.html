About

BSSPro is the go-to place for licensed educational curriculums, corporate training modules, and peer-reviewed scholarly articles/journals.

Award winning organization

BSS

Blockchain Student Society is a non-profit student led organization focused on educating, supporting, and providing an avenue for students to enter the Web3 Space.

We have partnered with educational institutions and have hosted numerous seminars, crypto meetups, social events, and more.

Learn More

Lamar University Pitch Battle Winner 2022

BSSPro

Blockchain Student Society Professional will be the world's first decentralized blockchain powered online education and research platform. We aim to simplify, standardize, & educate students on blockchain based technologies ranging from cryptocurrency to Decentralized Finance (Defi).

We offer educational resources for those ranging from higher education to corporations and a blockchain-powered journal.

Mission

M

The mission of BSSPro is to provide support to the ever changing and emerging blockchain space. By offering accredited courses and curriculums to higher education, consumers and corporations, and a peer-reviewed article, we hope to be the one-stop shop for all things blockchain. Interest in

Vision

V

BSSPro is a space to bring together people from all levels, from other institutions, and from the greater community in conversation about blockchain technology. BSSPro fosters innovation and talent in professionals and students to have a greater awareness of the need to transfer skills and knowledge across levels, disciplines, and experiences.

History

H

We began BSS in late 2021 in Lamar University, with Thanh Nguyen and Tien Nguyen as the Co-Founders. We aggregated a large community of like-minded people curious about this new technology. Then we entered into a start up pitch competition in September 2022 with the BSSPro concept, finishing as the 1st place winner.
